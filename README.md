## Detailed Notes

+ Paddle is ideal for SaaS businesses looking for a comprehensive solution that handles all aspects of selling digital products, including subscription management and global sales tax compliance.
+ Gumroad is a simple, creator-friendly platform best suited for small creators and artists, offering basic sales tools with no upfront costs but higher transaction fees.
+ Lemon Squeezy offers modern tools and features for creators, with competitive transaction fees and integrated marketing tools.
+ FastSpring provides robust features for global businesses with more complex needs, including advanced reporting, fraud protection, and subscription management.
Stripe is a highly customizable solution, best for developers who want full control over their payment processing and sales operations, with a wide range of integrations and APIs.

## How to choose the right platform?

+ **Transaction Fees**: Consider the percentage and any additional fees charged per transaction.
+ **Monthly Fees**: Check if there are any recurring charges beyond the transaction fees.
+ **Payout Frequency**: Determine how often you need to receive payments from your sales.
+ **International Sales**: Check if the platform supports international sales and how it handles localized payments and multi-currency transactions.
+ **Sales Tax Management**: Ensure the platform can handle the taxes applicable to your business and your customers.
+ **Merchant of Record**: Consider if you need the platform to act as your merchant of record.
+ **Chargeback Protection**: Check if the platform offers chargeback protection and how it handles disputes.
+ **Subscription Management**: Ensure the platform supports your subscription billing needs, including recurring payments and automated billing.
